BMI Calculator

Why BMI Matters

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in meters squared.

A BMI of 18.5 to 24.9 indicates a healthy weight. Below 18.5 is underweight, 25-29.9 is overweight, and 30 or higher indicates obesity.

While BMI is a useful screening tool, it doesn't directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass, age, sex, or ethnicity.

Calorie Intake Tracker

About Calorie Tracking

Calories are units of energy that our bodies use to function. The number of calories you need depends on factors like age, sex, weight, height, and activity level.

To maintain weight, you need to balance calories consumed with calories burned. To lose weight, you need a calorie deficit, and to gain weight, a calorie surplus.

This calculator estimates your daily calorie needs based on your personal information and goals.
